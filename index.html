    About Us...

    We put our customers and their needs first. Efficient business processes and a fruitful collaboration with suppliers allow us to ensure them a complete assortment, which combines quality, freshness and convenience. Furthermore, all our collaborators work to ensure a pleasant and functional shopping experience in Lidl stores.
    Our company has been present in Italy since 1992 and is part of the Schwarz Group, based in Neckarsulm in Germany: one of the leading retailers in the world. As a multinational company, we are aware of our responsibility towards our employees and business partners, the environment and the society in which we operate. For this reason, we are committed every day to promoting sustainable action.
